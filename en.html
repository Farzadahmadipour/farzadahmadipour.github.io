👤 About Me

Introduction

Ph.D. in Electrical Power Engineering with a research focus on multi-energy microgrids, energy storage, and the coordination of integrated power-gas networks. My academic activity includes serving as a reviewer for the international Journal of Energy Storage (Elsevier) and university lecturing experience at Islamic Azad University. Alongside this research and teaching path, executive experience in organizational management, information technology, and cybersecurity has given my work a practical, multidimensional perspective.

Strengths

  • Expertise in multi-energy microgrid coordination and optimization modeling with carbon emission considerations
  • Reviewer for the international Journal of Energy Storage
  • Teaching experience across Associate, Bachelor's, and Master's level courses at Islamic Azad University
  • Managerial experience leading an entrepreneurship institute and guiding executive teams and entrepreneurs
  • Operational familiarity with information technology and cybersecurity in the banking industry

Goals

Continued pursuit of applied research in integrating renewable energy resources and energy storage systems into power distribution networks, expanding international scientific collaborations, and transferring specialized knowledge to the next generation of engineers through university education and support for innovative energy-sector ventures.
