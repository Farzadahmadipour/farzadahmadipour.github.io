👤 About Me

Introduction

Dr. Farzad Ahmadipour Hedesh holds a Ph.D. in Electrical Power Engineering, with research focused on multi-energy microgrids, energy storage systems, and the coordination of integrated electricity and gas networks. His academic contributions include membership in the editorial board of the international journal Journal of Energy Storage (Elsevier) and a record of university teaching at Islamic Azad University. Alongside his research and academic career, practical experience in organizational management, information technology, and cybersecurity has given his professional profile a multifaceted, applied dimension.

Key Strengths

  • Expertise in multi-energy microgrids and optimization modelling with carbon emission considerations
  • Membership in the editorial review board of Journal of Energy Storage (Elsevier)
  • Teaching experience across associate, bachelor's, and master's degree programs at Islamic Azad University
  • Managerial experience leading an entrepreneurship institute and guiding executive teams
  • Hands-on knowledge of information technology and cybersecurity in the banking sector

Goals

Pursuing applied research on the integration of renewable energy sources and energy storage systems in electricity distribution networks, expanding international academic collaborations, and transferring specialized knowledge to the next generation of engineers through university teaching and support for innovative energy projects.
